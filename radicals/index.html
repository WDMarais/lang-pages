已经不是「笔画」了

这些不再是单笔，而是更大的零件：有的是只作偏旁的部件（亠）， 有的本身就是单字（二、大、人、入）。WaniKani 把笔画、部件、单字都叫「radical」。 These aren't single strokes any more — they're larger parts. Some are components that only ever sit inside other characters (亠); most are whole characters (二, 大, 人, 入) that also serve as radicals. Wanikani lumps strokes, components and characters all under "radical".

Here Wanikani's names mostly are the real meaning (大 = Big, 人 = Person) — so they carry across (green). Only the pure shape-mnemonics (亠 = Lid, 八 = Fins) don't (red). Stroke-order animation is deferred for these — the glyph is shown in Kai brush form.
