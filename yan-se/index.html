Modifiers修饰词

深与浅

浅 和 深 放在颜色词前面，表示颜色的深浅。 Put 浅 or 深 before any colour to shift it lighter or darker — one pattern, any colour.

浅
qiǎn
light (colour) · shallow
颜色浅 · 水浅
我想要浅蓝色的布料。
I'd like light-blue fabric.
这条河很浅。
This river is very shallow.
深
shēn
dark (colour) · deep
颜色深 · 水深
他穿了一件深绿色的西装。
He wore a dark-green suit.
这里的水很深。
The water here is very deep.

Character study字学

裁

裁

Composition字形

才
cái
phonetic — gives the sound
+
衣
yī
clothing — gives the domain
=
裁
cái
to cut (cloth/fabric)
才 (talent, cái) gives the pronunciation. 衣 (clothing, yī) grounds the meaning in the fabric domain — 裁 is specifically about cutting cloth, not cutting in general. The full top element 𢦏 also contains 戈 (a blade), reinforcing the cutting sense. 完整的上部 𢦏 包含戈（刀刃），进一步强调裁切的语义。
裁缝
cái féng
tailor · seamstress
做衣服的人
这里有一个很好的裁缝。
There's a very good tailor here.
裁剪
cái jiǎn
to cut · to trim
用剪刀剪布
师傅正在裁剪布料。
The master is cutting the fabric.
裁布
cái bù
to cut cloth
剪布料
先量尺寸，再裁布。
First measure, then cut the cloth.
